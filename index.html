Hi, I'm Ahan Gupta, a computer science PhD student at UIUC who is lucky to be advised by the great Minjia Zhang! Broadly, I investigate high-performance machine learning from the perspective of compilers/systems and algorithms. Check out my publications for my latest work.

In the not so distant past, I have worked as a researcher at Google DeepMind (Mountain View 2024), as well as a software engineer at Citadel (Hong Kong 2021) and Ninja-van (Singapore 2018). For more information, feel free to look at my cv.
